Project Outline

This is a p5js project with an arduino providing serial communication as input. The player controlls a spaceship with a joystick, and shoots lasers at the enemy with a button. Dodge the enemy laser by watching for the LED's on the arduino to flash!

Narrative Description

For this project, I generally wanted to make a simple game. I wanted to make a game that was easy to understand and play, which is why I made a game similar to asteroids. The game is as simple as it gets, you control a spaceship and you have to dodge the tracking laser. Originally, I had a "balance" mechanic, where the player had to tilt a potentiometer to balance the spaceship, but due to the potentiometer being low quality, it ended up not being fun. So, I removed the balance mechanic and just made the game easier. I wanted to iterate more with the game, but unfortunately I ran out of time.
